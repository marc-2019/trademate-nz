Common Questions

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. We'll send you a reset link. If you don't receive it within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact us at support@your-domain.co.nz.

Is BossBoard really free during beta?

Yes! During the beta period, all features are completely free for all users. No credit card required. When we move to paid tiers, we'll give you plenty of notice and you can choose to subscribe or continue on the free tier.

Are the AI-generated SWMS documents legally compliant?

Our SWMS generator is designed to align with WorkSafe NZ guidelines and the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015. However, AI-generated documents are starting points and must be reviewed by a competent person for your specific work situation. You remain responsible as the PCBU for ensuring all documentation is fit for purpose.

Can I invite my team members?

Absolutely! On the Team tier, you can invite up to 5 team members via email. They'll receive an invite link, create their own account, and join your team with the role you assign (Admin or Worker). During beta, team features are available to everyone.

How do I export or send an invoice?

Open any invoice and you'll see options to download as PDF, email directly to your customer, or share via a secure link. Your customer can view the invoice online through the client portal without needing a BossBoard account.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2/1.3) and we follow industry-standard security practices including JWT authentication, rate limiting, and regular backups. We comply with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Will you integrate with Xero?

Xero integration is on our roadmap for Q2-Q3 2026. It will include cashflow forecasting, automatic invoice syncing, and GST reporting. If you'd like to be notified when it's ready, let us know at support@your-domain.co.nz.
