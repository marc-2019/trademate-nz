Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 February 2026

1. Who We Are

TradeMate NZ is operated by Instilligent Limited, a New Zealand registered company. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users in accordance with the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand) and the Information Privacy Principles.

Contact: privacy@trademate.co.nz

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, trade type, business name, and business address when you register.
  • Business Data: Invoices, quotes, expenses, job logs, SWMS documents, certifications, and team member information you create within the app.
  • Photos: Receipt photos, site photos, and other images you upload to the app.
  • Payment Information: When you subscribe to a paid tier, payment is processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and push notification tokens for delivering app notifications.
  • Usage Data: Feature usage patterns to help us improve the app. This data is anonymised and aggregated.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the TradeMate NZ service
  • Generate compliance documents (SWMS) using AI assistance
  • Send invoices and quotes to your customers on your behalf
  • Send push notifications for certification expiry reminders
  • Process subscription payments through Stripe
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and support
  • Improve our service through anonymised usage analytics

4. AI-Powered Features

TradeMate NZ uses artificial intelligence (provided by Anthropic Claude) to generate compliance documentation such as Safe Work Method Statements (SWMS). When you use AI features:

  • Your job description and trade type are sent to the AI service to generate relevant documents
  • The AI does not retain or learn from your specific data after processing
  • Generated documents are stored only in your TradeMate NZ account
  • You are responsible for reviewing and verifying all AI-generated content before use

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe: For payment processing (when you subscribe to a paid tier)
  • Anthropic: For AI document generation (job descriptions and trade type only)
  • Your Customers: When you send invoices or share documents via the client portal
  • Your Team Members: When you invite team members, they can see shared business data as permitted by their role
  • Email Service Provider: For sending transactional emails (invoice delivery, verification codes)

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2/1.3) and at rest
  • JWT-based authentication with secure token handling
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Regular database backups
  • Role-based access controls for team features

7. Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a standard format
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@trademate.co.nz.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Personal account data is deleted within 30 days
  • Business documents (invoices, SWMS) may be retained for up to 7 years as required by New Zealand tax and compliance obligations
  • Anonymised usage data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement

9. Cookies

The TradeMate NZ mobile app does not use cookies. Our website (trademate.co.nz) uses only essential cookies for basic functionality and does not use third-party tracking cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@trademate.co.nz
  • Company: Instilligent Limited, New Zealand

12. Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response to a privacy concern, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner.
