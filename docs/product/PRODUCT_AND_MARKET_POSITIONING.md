## 1. Product Identity

### What Is TradeMate?

TradeMate NZ is a **mobile-first business management platform** purpose-built for New Zealand tradies and small service businesses. It combines AI-powered compliance documentation, invoicing, quoting, expense tracking, job logging, team management, and photo attachments into a single, affordable app.

### One-Line Pitch

> The all-in-one business app for NZ tradies - compliance, invoicing, and job management for less than a coffee a week.

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

TradeMate helps Kiwi tradies run their business from their phone. Generate safety docs with AI, send professional invoices, track expenses, log job hours, and manage your team - all in one app built specifically for New Zealand. Starting at less than $5 a week, it's the most affordable full-featured trade business platform in the market.

### Brand Values

- **Simple**: If it's not easy to use on a building site, it doesn't ship
- **Affordable**: Less than a coffee a week - the volume game, not the margin game
- **NZ-First**: Built for New Zealand regulations, GST, and business culture
- **Practical**: Solves real problems tradies face daily, not enterprise feature bloat

## 3. Problem Statement

### The Admin Burden

New Zealand tradies spend **5-10 hours per week** on business administration:
- Writing and chasing invoices
- Filling out safety paperwork (SWMS, risk assessments)
- Tracking certifications and license renewals
- Managing receipts and expenses
- Logging job hours and site visits
- Coordinating with team members

### The Compliance Anxiety

Under the **Health and Safety at Work Act 2015**, every PCBU (Person Conducting a Business or Undertaking) must:
- Identify and manage workplace hazards
- Maintain Safe Work Method Statements
- Track worker certifications and training
- Document risk assessments for each site

Non-compliance means **fines up to $600,000** and potential imprisonment. Most tradies know they should be doing more but don't have the time or tools.

### The Cash Flow Crisis

**51% of NZ SMEs** cite cash flow as their top business challenge:
- Late payments (average 52 days in NZ construction)
- No visibility into upcoming expenses
- GST surprises every two months
- Lost or forgotten invoices

### The Tool Gap

Existing solutions are either:
- **Too expensive**: Tradify ($30-50/mo), Fergus ($53-75/mo), Jobber ($50-100/mo)
- **Too complex**: Enterprise features that solo tradies don't need
- **Not NZ-specific**: US-centric platforms adapted poorly for NZ regulations
- **Missing key features**: No offline mode, no AI compliance, no photo attachments

## 5. Pricing Strategy

### Philosophy

> Less than a coffee a week. Cover costs through volume, not margin.

We price to **remove barriers to adoption**. At $4.99/week, TradeMate costs less than a flat white. The goal is **maximum market penetration** in the NZ tradie segment, building a large user base that generates strong recurring revenue at scale.

### Tier Structure

| | Free | Tradie | Team |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Price** | $0 | **$4.99 NZD/week** (~$19.99/mo) | **$9.99 NZD/week** (~$39.99/mo) |
| **Target** | Try before you buy | Solo operators | Small crews |
| Invoices | 3/month | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| SWMS | 2/month | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| PDF Export | - | Yes | Yes |
| Email Invoices | - | Yes | Yes |
| Quotes | - | Yes | Yes |
| Expenses | - | Yes | Yes |
| Job Logs | - | Yes | Yes |
| Photos | - | Yes | Yes |
| Team Members | - | - | Up to 5 |
| **GST** | - | Inclusive | Inclusive |

### Pricing Rationale

- **Free tier** exists to let tradies try the core product risk-free. Limited enough to drive upgrades, useful enough to demonstrate value.
- **Tradie at $4.99/wk** is the sweet spot: affordable for any sole trader, positioned well below all competitors, and covers our infrastructure costs with healthy margin at scale.
- **Team at $9.99/wk** adds multi-user for a modest uplift. Priced per-team (not per-user) which is simpler and more attractive than competitor per-seat models.
- **Weekly pricing** aligns with how tradies think about money (weekly pay cycles in NZ construction).

### Revenue Model

At target scale:
- 2,000 Tradie subscribers: ~$40,000/month
- 500 Team subscribers: ~$20,000/month
- **Total MRR target**: $60,000/month
- **Infrastructure cost**: <$2/user/month
- **Gross margin**: 85%+

## 8. Technology Differentiators

### AI Integration (Claude by Anthropic)

TradeMate uses Anthropic's Claude API to deliver AI features that no competitor can match:
- **SWMS Generation**: Input a job description, get a complete Safe Work Method Statement with NZ-specific hazards, control measures, and regulation references
- **Hazard Identification**: AI suggests workplace hazards based on trade type, job type, and site conditions
- **Control Measures**: Recommends controls following the hierarchy of controls (elimination > substitution > engineering > admin > PPE)
- **Smart Suggestions**: Context-aware auto-completion for compliance documents

### Architecture

| Component | Choice | Why |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| Mobile | React Native (Expo) | Single codebase for iOS + Android, rapid iteration |
| Backend | Node.js/Express + TypeScript | Fast development, strong typing, rich ecosystem |
| Database | PostgreSQL 16 | Reliable, scalable, JSON support for flexible schemas |
| Cache | Redis 7 | Session management, future job queues |
| AI | Claude API (Anthropic) | Best-in-class reasoning for compliance generation |
| Payments | Stripe NZ | NZ-native payment processing, weekly billing |
| Offline | SQLite (expo-sqlite) | True offline-first, sync when connected |
| PDF | PDFKit | Server-side PDF generation, no browser dependency |
