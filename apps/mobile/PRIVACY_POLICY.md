# Privacy Policy - BossBoard

**Last Updated: February 2026**

Instilligent Limited ("we", "us", or "our") operates the BossBoard mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Phone number (optional)
- Business name (optional)
- Trade type

### 1.2 Document Data
- SWMS documents you create
- Risk assessments
- Certification records
- Digital signatures

### 1.3 Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Crash reports and diagnostics

### 1.4 Usage Data
- Features used
- Documents created
- App performance metrics

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Generate compliance documents
- Store and sync your documents across devices
- Send certification expiry reminders
- Improve our services
- Provide customer support

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
- Documents are stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite
- You can use the App offline without sending data to our servers

### 3.2 Cloud Storage
- When online, documents sync to our secure servers in New Zealand
- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- We use industry-standard security practices

### 3.3 Data Retention
- Your data is retained while your account is active
- You can delete your account and all data at any time
- Backups are retained for 30 days after deletion

## 4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information:
- With your consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- With service providers who assist our operations (subject to confidentiality agreements)

### Service Providers
- Cloud hosting: AWS (Sydney region)
- AI processing: Anthropic (for hazard suggestions)
- Analytics: Privacy-focused analytics only

## 5. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Export your data
- Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@your-domain.co.nz

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the App or email.
