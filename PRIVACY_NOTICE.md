# Data Residency and Privacy Notice - TradeMate NZ

## Offshore Data Storage

Instilligent Limited operates TradeMate NZ on servers located in the United States through Railway.app infrastructure. New Zealand law (Privacy Act 2020, Information Privacy Principle 12) requires us to inform you about this offshore data storage and obtain your consent.

### Data Location
- Primary servers: United States (Railway.app datacentres)
- Reason: To ensure reliable, high-performance service delivery and uptime

### Data Categories
When you use TradeMate NZ, we may collect and store the following personal information:
- User names and contact details (email, phone, business address)
- Business and trade information
- Transaction and order records
- Payment and financial information
- Communication history and messages
- Service usage information and logs

### Safeguards
We implement appropriate technical and organisational safeguards:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments
- Data breach response procedures

### Your Rights and Choices
By using TradeMate NZ, you consent to your personal data being stored and processed offshore in the United States. You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
- Withdraw consent (may limit service functionality)

To exercise these rights or if you have privacy concerns, contact: **support@instilligent.com**

### Changes to This Notice
We may update this notice as needed. Continued use of the service after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated terms.
